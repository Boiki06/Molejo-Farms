About Molejo Farms

Molejo Farms is a family-owned agricultural business dedicated to bringing fresh, sustainable food and memorable farm experiences to our community.

We combine traditional farming practices with modern agricultural care to produce the best vegetables, livestock, event experiences, and farm stays.

Our Mission

To deliver nutritious, locally grown produce, offer agricultural education, and create enjoyable rural experiences for families and visitors.
